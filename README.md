## Privacy Policy

**Privacy Policy for Swaswa**

*Last Updated: February 5, 2025*

### Introduction

Welcome to Swaswa ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our fitness buddy finder application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using Swaswa, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

### Information We Collect

#### 1. Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Email address and password for account creation
- Display name and profile information
- Fitness level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
- Fitness interests (Running, Gym, Cycling, Yoga, Swimming, Hiking, CrossFit, Martial Arts, Dance, Tennis, Basketball, Soccer)
- Profile photo (optional)

**Fitness Data**
- Goals you create (workout targets, frequency)
- Activity logs and progress tracking
- Streak information

**Messages**
- Direct messages sent to connected workout buddies
- Connection requests

**Payment Information**
- If you subscribe to premium features, payment processing is handled securely by third-party providers (Apple App Store, Google Play Store)
- We do not store your payment card details

#### 2. Information Collected Automatically

**Location Data**
- Your geographic location to discover nearby workout buddies
- Location is used to calculate distance between users (within 10km radius)
- Location data is stored to enable the buddy discovery feature

**Usage Data**
- App features you use and how you interact with them
- Goals you create, view, or complete
- Connection activity and messaging frequency
- Session duration and frequency of use
- Device type, operating system, and app version

**Device Information**
- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Language and region settings

#### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not access your contacts, photos (except profile photo you upload), or other personal files
- We do not collect biometric data
- We do not collect health data from external devices or health apps
- We do not record audio or video

---

### How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Provide Services:** To connect you with nearby workout buddies based on location and shared interests
- **Enable Communication:** To facilitate messaging between connected users
- **Track Progress:** To maintain your fitness goals, streaks, and statistics
- **Improve the App:** To understand how users interact with our App and enhance features
- **Customer Support:** To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
- **Process Transactions:** To process in-app purchases and subscriptions
- **Display Advertisements:** To show relevant ads to free-tier users
- **Communications:** To send important updates about the App and notifications about connection requests and messages
- **Analytics:** To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience

### Data Storage and Security

**Storage**
- User data is stored securely on Firebase cloud servers
- Messages are stored to enable conversation history
- Location data is stored to enable buddy discovery
- User preferences are stored locally on your device

**Security Measures**
- All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
- Firebase provides enterprise-grade security
- Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only
- Regular security assessments are conducted
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text

**Data Retention**
- Account data is retained until you delete your account
- Messages are retained until you delete your account or the conversation
- Usage analytics are retained for up to 24 months
- Location data is updated when you use the app and retained with your profile

### Permissions We Request

**Location Access (Required)**
- Purpose: To find workout buddies near your location
- When Used: When you use the Discover feature to find nearby users
- Data Handling: Location is sent to our servers to calculate distances and show nearby users

**Push Notifications (Optional)**
- Purpose: To notify you of new connection requests, messages, and streak reminders
- When Used: When enabled, notifications are sent for relevant app events
- Data Handling: Notification tokens are stored to deliver push notifications

**Camera/Photo Library (Optional)**
- Purpose: To upload a profile photo
- When Used: Only when you choose to add or update your profile photo
- Data Handling: Photos are uploaded and stored on our servers for your profile

**Internet Access (Required)**
- Purpose: To sync data, fetch user profiles, and enable messaging
- When Used: Throughout app usage
- Data Handling: Encrypted transmission of all data
